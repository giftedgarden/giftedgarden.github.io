# Gifted Garden social redesign — owner approved

This review collection deliberately moves away from generated garden scenes and poster-style illustration.

## What changed

- Authentic provider photography is used with written permission.
- The owner confirms that written permission is on file for all child photographs supplied for public marketing.
- The approved elephant appears only as a controlled brand accent.
- Every post uses the same 80-pixel margin, typographic hierarchy, color system, and footer treatment.
- Script typography, fantasy scenery, excessive gradients, fake interface buttons, and changing mascot poses were removed.
- A single lavender-stem motif and a single golden sparkle form the supporting visual language.
- Feed designs use a 1080 × 1350, 4:5 format.
- Editable SVG masters are included beside the rendered PNG review files.

## Owner decisions — July 23, 2026

- Cleaner editorial direction: approved.
- Deeper purple and green palette: approved.
- Fredoka and Nunito Sans remain the working type system within the approved direction.
- “Known” language: not approved and removed. The provider graphic now says children are supported, respected, and encouraged to explore.
- Review destinations: confirmed for both Google and Yelp.
- Child and activity photographs: owner confirms written public-marketing permission for all supplied photographs.

## Publishing status

The five V2 designs are approved for publication. Time-sensitive availability or enrollment claims must still be reconfirmed on the day they are posted.
